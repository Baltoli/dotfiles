Privacy Policy

We are not affiliated, associated, authorized, endorsed by or in any way officially connected to Trello, Inc. (trello.com).

This Privacy Policy details how your private information is used by the following Browser extensions:

  • Boards for Trello for Google Chrome
  • New Tab Page for Trello for Google Chrome

When you authorise either of the above extensions for use with Trello, data made available from the Trello API is collected and registered with our servers.

  1. To check if you should be upgraded to the Pro version.
  2. To subscribe to our email newsletter, from which you can unsubscribe at any time.

Third party services used by the extension:

  • Trello, Inc, is used for obvious reasons. See https://trello.com/privacy
  • Google Analytics is used to monitor extension usage statistics
  • Stripe is used to collect payments for Pro Upgrades - https://stripe.com/au/privacy
  • See details regarding Google's privacy policy at https://www.google.com/policies/privacy/
