Set up document transfer

How should Dontprint transfer documents to your e-reader?
Choose this option if your e-reader is able to receive documents via e-mail attachment.
  1. Configure your e-reader to accept e-mails that are sent from "noreply@dontprint.net".
    Kindle users: Log in to Amazon's "Manage Your Content and Devices" page, go to the "Settings" tab, and add the address "noreply@dontprint.net" to the "Approved Personal Document E-mail List" (see image below; help):
  2. Tell Dontprint your e-reader's e-mail address (what's that?):
    If your e-reader is an Amazon Kindle then you find the e-mail address that is tied to your Kindle on Amazon's "Manage Your Content and Devices" page. Select the tab "Your Devices" and your Kindle's e-mail address will be displayed (see image below; further help). If your e-reader is not from Amazon then please consult the manual to find out if it can receive documents by e-mail.

Congratulations

You are now ready to use Dontprint. Go ahead and try it out:

  1. Go to the web page of a scientific article, e.g. this one.
  2. Wait for the page to load completely.
  3. A Dontprint icon should appear (see screenshot below). Click it!

Dontprint will download the PDF file of the article, optimize the layout for a small screen, and send it to your e-reader.
